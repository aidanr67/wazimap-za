About {{ WAZIMAP.name }}

The {{ WAZIMAP.name }} is an interactive web tool providing a range of information on young people, in one easily accessible place. The tool was developed in partnership with UCT's Poverty and Inequality Initiative, Western Cape Government, City of Cape Town, Stats SA and Code for South Africa.

With Thanks To

{{ WAZIMAP.name }} is based on Wazimap.co.za, a project by Media Monitoring Africa. Wazimap is open source and you can build one for your country.
